About Me

Academic profile, qualifications, and professional journey

Academic Biography

Dr. P. Chinnasamy is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering and brings extensive research experience in Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Blockchain Technology.

His research focuses on developing innovative solutions for intrusion detection systems, secure communication networks, and privacy-preserving AI frameworks. He has published numerous papers in reputed international journals and conferences, and actively contributes to the academic community as an IEEE and ACM member.

Dr. Chinnasamy is passionate about mentoring research scholars and guiding students to explore cutting-edge technologies. He supervises PhD scholars working on AI-based security systems, blockchain applications, and quantum cryptography. His teaching philosophy emphasizes hands-on learning and real-world problem solving.
